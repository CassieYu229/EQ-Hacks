Medicaid Qualification

Medicaid is a state funded insurance program aimed towards mainly low-income families and the elderly. Medicaid was founded to be able to insure low income families were able to access healthcare. Medicare in New York covers: regular exams, immunizations, doctor and clinic visits, relevant medical supplies and equipment, lab tests and x-rays, vision, dental, nursing home services, hospital stays, emergencies, and prescriptions.
