Wealth is Health

In such a big city, many NYC
residents face major health disparities.
The biggest health disparities most
heavily impact low income communities. These communities will often be surrounded by food desserts and have a lack of access to health care. “Wealth in Health” an NYC based project which seeks to help low-income families find hospitals and health facilities near them. Along with being able to help them identify how and if they can sign up for Medicaid.
